Shosha™ is a community-driven reputation and accountability platform operated by Builtattic Private Limited that enables users to discuss, report, react to, and engage with publicly referenced actions, conduct, statements, events, and social participation associated with individuals, organizations, public figures, and communities.

The platform is designed to facilitate participatory discourse, reputation signaling, public commentary, and community interpretation surrounding publicly observable conduct and socially relevant events.

Scores, reputation indicators, alignments, opposition metrics, credibility indicators, reports, and related platform signals are dynamic, community-influenced representations generated through platform participation, moderation systems, credibility weighting, algorithmic interpretation, and user-submitted content. These signals should not be interpreted as factual determinations, judicial findings, legal conclusions, or verified statements of truth.

Shosha™ does not claim to function as a court of law, governmental authority, investigative agency, or definitive verifier of all user-submitted information. User reports, opinions, interpretations, commentary, and submissions may contain inaccuracies, disputed claims, satire, criticism, subjective viewpoints, or unverified allegations.

The platform reserves the right to moderate, annotate, restrict, remove, review, deprioritize, or investigate content, profiles, reports, media, and user activity in order to maintain platform integrity, reduce abuse, comply with applicable laws, and improve user safety.

Users remain solely responsible for the content, reports, media, comments, and claims they submit or distribute through the platform.
