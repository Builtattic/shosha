This Report Appeals & Takedown Policy governs moderation review requests, appeals, profile disputes, content complaints, takedown requests, impersonation concerns, verification disputes, and related moderation procedures applicable to Shosha™, operated by Builtattic Private Limited.

This Policy operates in conjunction with the platform’s Terms of Service, Community Guidelines, Disclaimer & Reputation Policy, Verification & Trust Policy, Privacy Policy, and applicable law.

**1. Purpose of This Policy**

Shosha™ is a community-driven reputation and accountability platform that permits public discourse, user-generated reporting, commentary, criticism, reactions, alignments, opposition signals, and reputation participation involving publicly referenced individuals, organizations, communities, and events.

Because platform participation may involve disputed claims, subjective interpretations, criticism, or controversial discourse, Shosha™ provides moderation review and takedown mechanisms intended to:

- reduce abuse; 

- address impersonation concerns; 

- review harmful manipulation; 

- investigate fabricated evidence; 

- respond to legal complaints where appropriate; 

- improve moderation fairness; and 

- preserve platform integrity. 

Submission of an appeal, complaint, or takedown request does not guarantee removal, restoration, reinstatement, verification approval, or moderation reversal.

**2. Appeals Eligibility**

Users may request moderation review or appeal certain platform actions, including:

- content removals; 

- credibility penalties; 

- account suspensions; 

- verification denials; 

- trust badge revocations; 

- reporting restrictions; 

- visibility limitations; 

- moderation annotations; or 

- related enforcement actions. 

Appeals may be denied where:

- insufficient information is provided; 

- abuse patterns are reasonably suspected; 

- platform integrity concerns exist; 

- repeated violations occur; or 

- restoration would create operational, legal, or safety risks. 

**3. Takedown Requests**

Individuals, organizations, representatives, public figures, or authorized parties may submit requests involving:

- impersonation; 

- fabricated evidence; 

- manipulated media; 

- unlawful content; 

- doxxing or exposure of private information; 

- unauthorized explicit content; 

- copyright-related concerns; 

- safety threats; 

- fraud; 

- identity misuse; or 

- materially harmful platform abuse. 

Shosha™ reserves the right to:

- request supporting documentation; 

- request proof of authority; 

- request identity verification; 

- conduct internal moderation review; 

- annotate disputed material; 

- restrict visibility; 

- temporarily disable content; or 

- deny takedown requests 

where the platform reasonably determines that:

- public discourse protections apply; 

- claims remain disputed; 

- insufficient evidence exists; 

- the request is abusive or fraudulent; or 

- removal is not operationally or legally required. 

**4. Public Figure and Reputation Disputes**

Shosha™ recognizes that reports, commentary, and reputation-related participation involving public figures, organizations, or recognizable individuals may involve criticism, disagreement, satire, disputed claims, or controversial public discourse.

The platform does not guarantee the removal of content solely because:

- a report is unfavorable; 

- criticism is uncomfortable; 

- commentary is unpopular; 

- discourse is controversial; or 

- reputation indicators are disputed. 

However, the platform may review or restrict content where reasonably necessary to address:

- fabricated evidence; 

- impersonation; 

- coordinated harassment; 

- unlawful threats; 

- malicious manipulation; 

- exploitative conduct; 

- platform abuse; or 

- legal compliance obligations.

Users claiming ownership of profiles, public figure accounts, organizational profiles, or identity-related features may be required to provide:

- government-issued identification; 

- platform verification materials; 

- proof of representation; 

- ownership documentation;

Verification decisions are made at the platform’s discretion and may consider:

- authenticity indicators; 

- moderation history; 

- impersonation concerns; 

- fraud risks; 

- operational integrity; 

- abuse-prevention considerations; and 

- platform safety requirements. 

Verification approval is not guaranteed.
