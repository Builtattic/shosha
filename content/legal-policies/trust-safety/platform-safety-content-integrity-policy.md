**Shosha****™**

**Effective Date: ****1****st**** June****, 2026**

This Platform Safety & Content Integrity Policy governs platform safety standards, abuse prevention systems, harmful conduct restrictions, manipulation controls, integrity enforcement mechanisms, media authenticity standards, and operational safety measures applicable to Shosha™, operated by Builtattic Private Limited.

This Policy operates in conjunction with the platform’s Terms of Service, Community Guidelines, Privacy Policy, Verification & Trust Policy, Disclaimer & Reputation Policy, and applicable laws.

**1. Purpose of This Policy**

Shosha™ is designed to support community-driven discourse, public commentary, reputation participation, accountability discussions, and user-generated reporting involving publicly referenced conduct, events, organizations, communities, and public figures.

Because the platform involves:

- reputation systems; 

- public participation; 

- credibility indicators; 

- user-generated allegations; 

- anonymous reporting functionality; and 

- community interaction mechanisms, 

Shosha™ maintains platform integrity and safety systems intended to:

- reduce abuse; 

- discourage manipulation; 

- limit coordinated harassment; 

- preserve platform reliability; 

- discourage misinformation abuse; 

- improve moderation consistency; and 

- maintain operational stability. 

**2. Harmful Conduct Restrictions**

Users must not use the platform to:

- coordinate harassment campaigns; 

- encourage mob targeting; 

- threaten violence or unlawful harm; 

- publish doxxing material or sensitive personal information; 

- engage in stalking or intimidation; 

- encourage self-harm or suicide; 

- distribute exploitative or abusive material; 

- target minors with inappropriate content; 

- promote unlawful conduct; 

- impersonate others deceptively; or 

- intentionally destabilize platform operations. 

The platform reserves the right to remove or restrict content that creates credible risks involving:

- user safety; 

- coordinated abuse; 

- manipulation campaigns; 

- unlawful exploitation; 

- platform integrity threats; or 

- operational disruption. 

**3. Brigading and Coordinated Manipulation**

Shosha™ prohibits coordinated attempts to artificially manipulate:

- reputation systems; 

- credibility indicators; 

- reporting systems; 

- alignments or opposition activity; 

- visibility mechanisms; 

- moderation outcomes; or 

- trust systems. 

Prohibited manipulation may include:

- mass-targeting campaigns; 

- coordinated false reporting; 

- artificial amplification; 

- bot-assisted engagement; 

- spam behavior; 

- reputation farming; 

- vote brigading; 

- fraudulent verification attempts; or 

- coordinated credibility abuse. 

The platform may apply:

- participation limits; 

- visibility restrictions; 

- credibility penalties; 

- trust reductions; 

- moderation review; 

- account suspensions; or 

- permanent bans 

where manipulation or coordinated abuse is reasonably suspected.

**4. Fabricated Evidence and Media Manipulation**

Users must not knowingly submit:

- fabricated screenshots; 

- misleading edits; 

- manipulated evidence; 

- falsified documentation; 

- synthetic impersonation material; 

- deceptive AI-generated media; or 

- altered content intended to mislead users, moderators, or platform systems. 

AI-generated or manipulated content used deceptively within reports, allegations, credibility disputes, or moderation-sensitive contexts may result in:

- immediate content removal; 

- credibility penalties; 

- verification restrictions; 

- trust revocation; 

- account suspension; or 

- permanent removal from the platform. 

Shosha™ may utilize automated systems, moderation tooling, integrity systems, or human review processes to investigate suspected manipulation.

**5. Child Safety and Exploitative Content**

Shosha™ maintains zero tolerance for:

- exploitative content involving minors; 

- unlawful sexual material involving children; 

- grooming-related conduct; 

- coercive exploitation; 

- abusive imagery involving minors; or 

- unlawful child-targeting behavior. 

The platform reserves the right to:

- immediately remove related content; 

- suspend associated accounts; 

- preserve operational records; 

- cooperate with lawful authorities; and 

- implement emergency safety measures 

where child safety concerns reasonably arise.

Users must not misuse:

- verification systems; 

- trust badges;
